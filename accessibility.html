Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: June 1, 2026  |  CTF Designs  |  Murrieta, CA

♿ Our Commitment

CTF Designs is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually working to improve the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards to achieve this goal.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as defined by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

We consider our work "accessibility-minded." We follow many WCAG 2.1 Level AA practices and build with accessibility in mind, but we do not claim full conformance or that any site is fully ADA compliant. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and full legal compliance depends on factors beyond design alone.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of this website relies on the following technologies to work with browser and assistive technology combinations:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript (vanilla)

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Measures We Have Taken

CTF Designs takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of our website:

  • Semantic HTML elements are used throughout to communicate structure and meaning
  • All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard navigation
  • ARIA labels and roles are applied to navigation, buttons, and form elements
  • Form inputs include associated labels
  • Color contrast ratios meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA minimums for text
  • Interactive elements have visible focus states
  • Images and decorative elements use appropriate alt attributes or are marked as decorative (aria-hidden="true")
  • The site is responsive and functional across device sizes and orientations
  • Skip navigation and landmark regions are used to aid screen reader users

Known Limitations

Despite our efforts, there may be some areas of our website that are not yet fully accessible. We are actively working to identify and address these issues. Known areas under review include:

  • Some animated background elements, decorative blobs are marked with aria-hidden to avoid screen reader confusion
  • Portfolio project previews, text alternatives are provided for all images and visual content

We welcome feedback on any accessibility issues you encounter and will work to address them promptly.

Built with Accessibility in Mind

CTF Designs builds with accessibility in mind and we are what we'd call "accessibility-minded." We believe people with disabilities deserve equal access to digital information, and we apply accessibility best practices in our work. We do not, however, promise or guarantee that this site or the sites we build are fully compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) or any specific standard.

Assistive Technologies Supported

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Browser zoom (up to 200% without loss of content or functionality)
  • High contrast mode
  • Voice control software
